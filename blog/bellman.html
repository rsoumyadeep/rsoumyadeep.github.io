Understanding the Bellman Equation

April 2026 · Reinforcement Learning

The Bellman equation defines the value of a state recursively.

Mathematical Form

$$ V(s) = \max_a \left[ R(s,a) + \gamma \mathbb{E}[V(s')] \right] $$

Intuition

It decomposes a complex decision problem into smaller subproblems.
