🎮 Trajectory

🚀 Policy Learning

🎯 Objective

\[ J(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{\tau \sim \pi_\theta}[R(\tau)] \]

📐 Policy Gradient

\[ \nabla_\theta J = \mathbb{E}[\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a|s) R] \]

⚡ Variance Reduction

🌪 Entropy

\[ J(\theta) = \mathbb{E}[R] + \beta H(\pi_\theta) \]

🔥 PPO

\[ L^{CLIP} = \mathbb{E}[\min(r_t A_t, clip(r_t,1-\epsilon,1+\epsilon)A_t)] \]

📉 GAE

\[ A_t^{GAE} = \sum_{l=0}^{\infty} (\gamma \lambda)^l \delta_{t+l} \]

📚 Full Derivation

\[ J(\theta) = \int P(\tau;\theta)R(\tau)d\tau \] \[ \nabla_\theta J = \int \nabla_\theta P(\tau;\theta)R(\tau)d\tau \] \[ = \int P(\tau)\nabla_\theta \log P(\tau)R(\tau)d\tau \] \[ = \mathbb{E}[\nabla_\theta \log P(\tau)R(\tau)] \] \[ \log P(\tau)=\sum_t \log \pi_\theta(a_t|s_t) \] \[ \Rightarrow \nabla_\theta J = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t \nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a_t|s_t)R(\tau)\right] \]
